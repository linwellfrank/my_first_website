Top 3 Movies Watched

By Linwell Van Wyk

  1. Superman

    ★★★★★

    A timeless tale of a Kryptonian orphan raised on Earth, Superman embodies the ultimate hero. Possessing extraordinary strength, speed, and invulnerability, he protects Metropolis and the world from countless threats.

  2. Batman

    ★★★★☆

    The Dark Knight of Gotham City, Batman is a vigilante who uses fear to fight crime. Armed with cutting-edge technology and a relentless pursuit of justice, he is a symbol of hope in a city often shrouded in darkness.

  3. Hitman

    ★★★★½

    A professional assassin known as Agent 47, Hitman is a genetically engineered clone with a barcode on his neck. His deadly skills and cold demeanor make him a formidable force, but he is often caught in a web of intrigue and betrayal.
